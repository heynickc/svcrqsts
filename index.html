Since the introduction of the Cityworks Maintenance Management System to Salisbury Public Works in late 2009, the department's Water and Sewer Utilities Branch and Operations Division has recorded almost 9,000 requests for service for the various services that are provided for citizens.

This map is a visualization of those Service Requests that were recorded with an address that was able to be located using geographic information systems (GIS).
